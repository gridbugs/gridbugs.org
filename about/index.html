About

I’m Stephen Sherratt. I live in Sydney, Australia and I write software for a living. I’m obsessed with modular synthesizers and technology from the 80s. I use he/him pronouns. Sometimes I make games. I cycle a lot, and enjoy camping and hiking, though I don’t post much about that here.

For a long time my main hobby was roguelike development. I’ve entered the 7 Day Roguelike every year since 2016. This site started as a place for posting development logs for various game projects. Nowadays I post about more general programming and electronics projects I work on in my spare time.

For more things I’ve worked on outside of this site, see the External Links page.

To contact me about this site, see the Contact page.
