Getting back into bouldering

October 04, 2020

During the peak of covid in NSW I stopped bouldering. Yesterday I climbed for the first time in about 3 months. My muscle memory still remembers how to climb, but my muscles themselves, unsurprisingly, are struggling, especially my forearms. I also seem to tire much more quickly than before. This will pass. I’m going to start climbing weekly.
